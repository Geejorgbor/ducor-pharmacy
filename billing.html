Billing & Payment

Home › Billing & Payment

Ducor International Pharmacy is committed to making your payment process simple and secure. We accept multiple payment methods for customers ordering from anywhere in the world. Your medication will be ready for pickup at our pharmacy in Monrovia, Liberia. Follow the instructions below for your preferred payment method.

How to Pay Your Bill

MTN Mobile Money (Lonestar)

Send payment directly from your MTN account

Liberia
Send To (Number)+231 88 722 1275
Account NameDucor International Pharmacy
NetworkLonestar MTN
1

Dial *156# on your Lonestar MTN line or open your MTN MoMo app.

2

Select "Send Money" and enter the number +231 88 722 1275.

3

Enter the exact total amount shown on your order confirmation.

4

Confirm the transaction and save or screenshot your receipt.

5

WhatsApp your receipt to +231 887 221 275 with your order reference number to confirm your order.

Note: Only the Lonestar MTN number has a Mobile Money account. Please do not send to any other number. Once we confirm your payment, your order will be processed and ready for pickup.

Sendwave

International money transfer — fast & fee-free

International
Send To (Phone)+231 88 722 1275
Recipient NameDucor International Pharmacy
CountryLiberia 🇱🇷
1

Download the Sendwave app on your phone (available on iOS & Android) or go to sendwave.com.

2

Create an account or log in, then select Liberia as the destination country.

3

Enter the phone number +231 88 722 1275 as the recipient.

4

Enter the exact total amount from your order confirmation and complete the transfer.

5

WhatsApp your receipt or transfer confirmation to +231 887 221 275 with your order reference number.

Why Sendwave? Sendwave offers zero-fee transfers to Liberia from the USA, UK, Canada, and many other countries. It is fast, secure, and widely used.

Cash on Delivery / Pickup

Pay when you collect your order in Liberia

Free
Location10 & 11 Street, Tubman Boulevard
LandmarkNear Ecobank, Monrovia
HoursMon – Sat, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM
1

Place your order online and select "Cash on Delivery" as your payment method.

2

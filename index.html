About Us

Liberia's Premier Healthcare Hub

Ducor International Pharmacy specializes in the international distribution of FDA-approved medication and medical supplies. We are steadfast advocates for ensuring Liberians have access to high-quality and safe medication.

As an affiliate of PharMed Consultants Liberia, INC, our team of doctors, pharmacists, and medical professionals work together to deliver exceptional, high-quality care you can trust.
